Setting Up Email Templates

  1. 1

    Connect to FTP

    Locate your active theme. For example, 

    app/design/frontend/[themeVendor]/[theme]/
  2. 2

    Upload Files via FTP

    Upload the content of the email template folder (Magento_Contact, Magento_Customer, Magento_Email, etc.) to the folder of your active theme. For example, 

    app/design/frontend/[themeVendor]/[theme]/Magento_Contact/
    app/design/frontend/[themeVendor]/[theme]/Magento_Customer/
    app/design/frontend/[themeVendor]/[theme]/Magento_Email/
    etc.
  3. 3

    Clear the Cache through the Admin Panel

    Go to System > Tools > Cache Management and make sure to flush all caches.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do you mean by “one purchase for all your future Magento based projects”?

At least four times per year, we come with new email template designs & bug-fix updates. We want to build a community around email templates and offer this community our other solutions.

What is included?

All standard transactional emails are included. If you are using a third-party module to send emails, then you will have to ask those developers how to set their emails.

Can I use all 20 styles?

Yes, but keep in mind that a website can use only one active email template at a time.

Is it a module?

No, it is a design. Think about email templates as a Magento theme but for emails.

Do I need coding skills?

Basic customization, like changing the logo, text, and colors, can be done from your Magento admin panel. For more advanced customization, you will need some basic HTML & CSS skills.
